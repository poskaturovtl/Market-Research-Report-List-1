<p><p>The head gasket market is categorized into four primary types: Multi-Layer Steel (MLS), Solid Copper, Composite, and Elastomeric. MLS gaskets are known for durability, featuring multiple steel layers that provide a robust seal. Solid Copper gaskets deliver excellent thermal conductivity but may require regular maintenance. Composite gaskets combine materials for flexibility and are commonly used in various applications. Elastomeric gaskets offer excellent sealing properties, often used in high-performance engines due to their resilience and adaptability under extreme conditions.</p></p>
