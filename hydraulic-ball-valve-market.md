<p><p>A hydraulic ball valve is a type of valve that uses a spherical disc, or ball, to control the flow of hydraulic fluid in a system. The ball has a hollow center that allows fluid to flow through when the valve is open and blocks the flow when closed. These valves are known for their reliability, durability, and ability to maintain consistent flow rates under high-pressure conditions, making them essential in various hydraulic applications across industries, including oil and gas, manufacturing, and construction.</p><p>The Hydraulic Ball Valve Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for automation and efficient fluid control systems. Directional valves control the flow path of hydraulic fluids, enabling precise movement in machinery and equipment. Collectively, these valves enhance efficiency and safety in hydraulic systems across diverse industries, including manufacturing and automotive.</p></p>
