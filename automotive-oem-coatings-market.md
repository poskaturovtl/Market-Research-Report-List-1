<p><strong>The Automotive OEM Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Borne</li><li>Waterborne</li><li>Powdered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive OEM Coatings market includes three primary types: solvent-borne, waterborne, and powdered coatings. Solvent-borne coatings utilize organic solvents, offering good adhesion and durability but face regulatory challenges due to VOC emissions. Waterborne coatings use water as a solvent, providing an eco-friendlier option with lower emissions and easier application processes. Powdered coatings are applied as dry powder and cured under heat, resulting in a durable finish and minimal waste, making them an efficient, environmentally-conscious choice for automotive manufacturers.</p></p>
