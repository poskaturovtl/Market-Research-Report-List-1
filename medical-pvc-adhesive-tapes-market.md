<p><p>The medical PVC adhesive tapes market is categorized into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided tapes feature adhesive on one side, suitable for securing dressings or medical devices to the skin. They offer ease of use and targeted application. In contrast, double-sided tapes have adhesive on both sides, allowing for stronger bond strength between surfaces, commonly used in prosthetics and for attaching sensors or devices. Each type meets specific medical needs, enhancing patient care and ensuring effective treatment delivery.</p></p>
