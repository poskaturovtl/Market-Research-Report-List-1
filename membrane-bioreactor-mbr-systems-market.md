<p><p>Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) systems utilize biological treatment combined with membrane filtration to effectively purify wastewater. The market for MBRs is segmented into three main types: Hollow Fiber, Flat Sheet, and Multi Tubular membranes. Hollow Fiber systems feature bundled, thin fibers that maximize surface area, suited for compact designs. Flat Sheet membranes utilize a large, planar surface for filtration, often enhancing ease of cleaning. Multi Tubular designs consist of tubes that offer high flow rates, making them ideal for larger-scale applications. Each type addresses different operational needs and efficiency requirements in wastewater treatment.</p></p>
