<p><p>The metallic flange gasket sheet market is categorized into two main types: flat flange and non-planar flange gaskets. Flat flange gaskets are designed for uniform, even surfaces, ensuring effective sealing in applications with parallel flanges. Non-planar flange gaskets, on the other hand, accommodate irregular or uneven surfaces, providing adaptability and enhanced sealing performance in complex assemblies. Both types are crucial in various industries to prevent leaks, maintain pressure, and ensure the integrity of piping systems and equipment.</p></p>
