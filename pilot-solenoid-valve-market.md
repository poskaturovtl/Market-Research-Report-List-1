<p><strong>The Pilot Solenoid Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Way Solenoid Valve</li><li>Three-Way Solenoid Valve</li><li>Four-Way Solenoid Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pilot solenoid valve market comprises three main types: two-way, three-way, and four-way valves. Two-way solenoid valves control fluid flow in a single direction, making them suitable for basic on/off applications. Three-way solenoid valves manage flow between two ports, allowing for mixing or redirecting fluids, ideal for automation systems. Four-way solenoid valves facilitate the operation of double-acting cylinders by controlling the flow to and from the actuator, essential in more complex pneumatic or hydraulic systems.</p></p>
