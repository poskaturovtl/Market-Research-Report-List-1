<p><strong>The Stationary CT Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-slice CT</li><li>Mid-slice CT</li><li>Low-slice CT</li><li>Cone-beam CT (CBCT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary CT scanner market is categorized into High-slice, Mid-slice, Low-slice, and Cone-beam CT (CBCT) scanners. High-slice CT scanners, typically 64 slices and above, offer rapid imaging and detailed cross-sectional views. Mid-slice CT scanners, with around 16 to 64 slices, provide a balance between image quality and acquisition speed. Low-slice CT scanners, generally up to 16 slices, are often utilized for simpler diagnostic tasks. CBCT systems, mainly used in dental and orthopedic applications, deliver 3D imaging with a lower radiation dose.</p></p>
