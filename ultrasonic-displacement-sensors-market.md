<p><p>Ultrasonic displacement sensors are available in two main types: analog and digital. Analog ultrasonic sensors provide continuous voltage or current output proportional to the measured distance, making them suitable for applications requiring real-time monitoring. In contrast, digital ultrasonic sensors convert distance measurements into digital signals, offering precise readings and easier integration with digital systems. Both types are widely used in automation, robotics, and object detection, serving diverse industries with their unique advantages in accuracy and versatility.</p></p>
