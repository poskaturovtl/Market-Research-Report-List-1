<p><p>Cardiopulmonary autotransfusion systems (ATS) are medical devices used to collect and reinfuse a patient's own blood during surgical procedures. The market for ATS is primarily divided into two types: unwashed ATS and washed ATS. Unwashed ATS systems collect blood without any processing, allowing for rapid reinfusion, while washed ATS systems involve a cleaning process to remove impurities and minimize the risk of transfusion reactions. Both are critical in enhancing patient safety and recovery during surgery.</p></p>
