<p><p>The Aluminum Matrix Composite (AMC) market is categorized into three main types: continuous, discontinuous, and particle-reinforced composites. Continuous composites utilize long, continuous fibers for enhanced strength and stiffness, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. Discontinuous composites incorporate short fibers, providing a balance of performance and cost, suitable for structural applications. Particle-reinforced composites feature particles dispersed in the aluminum matrix, enhancing wear resistance and thermal properties, commonly used in electrical and thermal applications. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to specific industry needs.</p></p>
