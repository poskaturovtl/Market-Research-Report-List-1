<p><strong>The Mechanical Torque Limiter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball Torque Limiter</li><li>Friction Torque Limiter</li><li>Pneumatic Torque Limiter</li><li>Push/Pull Torque Limiter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical torque limiter market is segmented into four main types: </p><p>1. **Ball Torque Limiter:** Utilizes ball bearings to disengage when a preset torque is exceeded, protecting machinery.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Friction Torque Limiter:** Employs friction surfaces that slip at a specific torque level to prevent overload.</p><p>3. **Pneumatic Torque Limiter:** Uses air pressure to regulate torque, providing precise control in various applications.</p><p>4. **Push/Pull Torque Limiter:** Functions through mechanical engagement or disengagement based on tension, effectively managing torque levels in critical systems.</p></p>
