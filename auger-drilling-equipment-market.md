<p><strong>The Auger Drilling Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Flight Augers</li><li>Bucket Augers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auger Drilling Equipment market features two primary types: Continuous Flight Augers and Bucket Augers. Continuous Flight Augers utilize a helical screw design to excavate soil continuously, making them ideal for deep drilling applications, such as foundation and geotechnical work. Bucket Augers, on the other hand, consist of a bucket at the end of the drill for scooping and removing materials, suited for shallower depths and various soil types. Both are essential for effective drilling operations in construction and environmental projects.</p></p>
