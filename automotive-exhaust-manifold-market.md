<p><strong>The Automotive Exhaust Manifold Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Wall</li><li>Double wall</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive exhaust manifold market primarily consists of two types: single wall and double wall manifolds. Single wall manifolds feature a straightforward design with a single layer, providing efficient exhaust flow but less thermal insulation. In contrast, double wall manifolds consist of an outer and inner layer, which help reduce heat transfer to the engine components, enhancing performance and fuel efficiency. The choice between them often depends on vehicle requirements, thermal management, and overall design considerations.</p></p>
