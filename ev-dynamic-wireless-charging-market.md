<p><p>The EV dynamic wireless charging market encompasses various technologies for transmitting power to electric vehicles while in motion. Inductive Power Transfer (IPT) uses electromagnetic fields to transfer energy between coils. Capacitive Power Transfer (CPT) employs electric fields between capacitive plates for energy transfer. Magnetic Power Transfer utilizes magnetic fields to convey energy effectively. Resonance Inductive Power Transfer (RIPT) allows efficient energy transfer over greater distances by using resonant frequencies, enabling seamless charging of EVs without direct contact.</p></p>
