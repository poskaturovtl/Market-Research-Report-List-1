<p><p>The uniform particle size ion exchange resins market is categorized into four main types: strong acid, weak acid, strong base, and weak base resins. Strong acid resins effectively remove cations by providing protons, while weak acid resins allow for the exchange of protons at lower pH levels. Strong base resins attract anions by releasing hydroxide ions, whereas weak base resins exchange anions with less aggressive conditions. Each type finds applications across water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and chemical processing, addressing specific ion removal needs.</p></p>
