<p><p>The biliary catheters market encompasses various applications essential for managing biliary conditions. Occlusion catheters block abnormal flow, and infusion catheters deliver medications or fluids directly. Aspirating catheters aid in sample collection or fluid removal, while dialysis catheters support kidney treatment.
