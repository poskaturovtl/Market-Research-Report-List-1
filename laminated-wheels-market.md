<p><p>The Laminated Wheels market is categorized into two main types: bonded and non-bonded. Bonded type laminated wheels feature a strong adhesive that securely binds the layers together, providing enhanced durability and performance for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, non-bonded type laminated wheels consist of individual layers that are simply stacked, allowing for easier customization and flexibility but may offer less stability under intense conditions. Both types cater to different user needs across various industrial applications.</p></p>
