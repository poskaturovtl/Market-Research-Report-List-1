<p><strong>The Flat Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronically-steered Antenna</li><li>Mechanically-steered Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat antennas market is primarily divided into electronically-steered and mechanically-steered antennas. Electronically-steered antennas utilize electronic systems to adjust their beam direction rapidly, making them suitable for dynamic tracking applications such as communications and radar. In contrast, mechanically-steered antennas rely on physical movements to direct the antenna's orientation, resulting in slower adjustments but often offering simpler designs. Both types cater to various industries, including telecommunications, aerospace, and defense, driven by the demand for compact, efficient communication solutions.</p></p>
