<p><p>The EV AC charging station market consists of three primary types: Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3. Level 1 chargers are basic household outlets, providing slow charging, ideal for overnight use. Level 2 chargers offer faster charging, suitable for residential and commercial installations, and typically require a dedicated circuit. Level 3 chargers, also known as DC fast chargers, deliver rapid charging and are primarily found in public charging stations, catering to drivers needing quick recharges during long trips.</p></p>
