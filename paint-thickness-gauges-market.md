<p><p>The paint thickness gauges market primarily consists of two technologies: magnetic and eddy current, along with ultrasonic technology. Magnetic and eddy current gauges are suited for measuring non-destructive coatings on metal surfaces, where magnetic gauges work on ferrous substrates and eddy current gauges on non-ferrous metals. Ultrasonic technology, however, is versatile, working on various substrates, including plastics and composites. Each technology has its application areas, catering to diverse industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, ensuring precise measurements for quality control.</p></p>
