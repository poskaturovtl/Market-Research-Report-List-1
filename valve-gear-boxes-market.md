<p><p>Valve gearboxes are essential components in various industrial applications, providing precise control of valve operations. The market can be categorized into two main types: worm gear and bevel gear. Worm gears offer high torque and excellent gear reduction, ideal for applications requiring significant force in a compact design. In contrast, bevel gears facilitate the transfer of motion at different angles, allowing for versatile configurations in tight spaces. Both types enhance operational efficiency and reliability in fluid control systems.</p></p>
