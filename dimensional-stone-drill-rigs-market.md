<p><ul><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dimensional Stone Drill Rigs market encompasses two primary types: hydraulic and mechanical rigs. Hydraulic drill rigs utilize fluid pressure to power the drilling process, offering greater efficiency and control, particularly in challenging rock formations. They are known for their versatility and ability to handle various drill sizes. In contrast, mechanical drill rigs operate using traditional rotary methods, relying on physical components such as gears and levers. They are typically simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for less complex drilling tasks.</p></p>
