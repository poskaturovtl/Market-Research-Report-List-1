<p><strong>The Light Grids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diffraction Light Grids</li><li>Reflex Light Grids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Light Grids market comprises primarily two types: Diffraction Light Grids and Reflex Light Grids. Diffraction Light Grids utilize the principles of diffraction to control light patterns, making them ideal for applications in spectroscopy and optical testing. In contrast, Reflex Light Grids rely on reflective surfaces to redirect light beams, enhancing imaging systems and optical sensors. Both types serve diverse industries including telecommunications, manufacturing, and healthcare, advancing technologies in light manipulation and sensor accuracy.</p></p>
