<p><p>Automotive crash sensors are crucial for vehicle safety systems, detecting and responding to collisions. Pressure sensors monitor changes in compartment pressure, while speed sensors assess impact severity based on vehicle speed. Temperature sensors help identify overheating conditions after a crash. Gas sensors detect harmful gases, ensuring passenger safety post-accident. Level sensors gauge fluid levels to avoid spills, and position sensors determine the exact location of components during a crash, aiding in airbag deployment and overall vehicle safety management.</p></p>
