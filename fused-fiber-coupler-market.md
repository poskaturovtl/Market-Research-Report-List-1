<p><strong>The Fused Fiber Coupler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Couplers</li><li>Multimode Couplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fused Fiber Coupler market comprises two main types: Single Mode Couplers and Multimode Couplers. Single Mode Couplers are designed for use with single mode fibers, enabling efficient signal transmission over long distances with minimal loss and crosstalk, making them ideal for telecommunications. Multimode Couplers, on the other hand, are suited for multimode fibers, supporting multiple light paths and suitable for applications requiring shorter distances in data centers and local area networks, offering higher coupling efficiency.</p></p>
