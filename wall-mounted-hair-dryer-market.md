<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Hair Dryer</li><li>Axial Hair Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wall-mounted hair dryer market comprises two main types: centrifugal and axial hair dryers. Centrifugal hair dryers utilize a fan to draw air in and expel it at high speeds, providing strong airflow and quick drying times. Axial hair dryers, on the other hand, direct air flow along the axis of the unit, offering a more focused blast suitable for styling. Both types are designed for space-saving convenience, often found in hotels and gyms, enhancing user experience and efficiency.</p></p>
