<p><strong>The Turbo Expander Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial-Axial Turbo Expander</li><li>Radial Turbo Expander</li><li>Axial Turbo Expander</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turbo expander market is categorized primarily into three types: radial-axial turbo expanders, radial turbo expanders, and axial turbo expanders. Radial-axial turbo expanders combine elements of both designs, offering efficient performance across diverse applications. Radial turbo expanders utilize a centrifugal force mechanism for gas expansion, emphasizing compact design and high efficiency. In contrast, axial turbo expanders facilitate gas flow in a linear direction, ideal for large-scale applications where high volume and continuous operation are crucial. Each type serves specific industry needs.</p></p>
