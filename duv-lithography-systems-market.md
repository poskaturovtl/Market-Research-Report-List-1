<p><ul><li>ArF Dry Excimer Systems ­</li><li>ArF Immersion Excimer Systems ­</li><li>KrF Excimer Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DUV lithography systems market is categorized into three main types based on the wavelength used for semiconductor manufacturing. ArF Dry Excimer Systems utilize a 193 nm wavelength in a dry environment, ideal for standard process nodes. ArF Immersion Excimer Systems employ the same wavelength but in an immersion mode, enhancing resolution for advanced nodes. KrF Excimer Systems operate at a longer 248 nm wavelength, suitable for older technologies. Each type addresses specific needs in the semiconductor fabrication process.</p></p>
